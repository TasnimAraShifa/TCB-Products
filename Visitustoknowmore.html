TCB Product System is an online system where customer can apply for pre-book the TCB product in online. This project is intended to make the save the time, proper full fill daily need, not facing terrifying situation to standing front of the truck hour and hour. A huge number of people deepened in this service to lead their life and most of the time they are victim of many terrifying cases. The total processes of managing this system are useful and time consuming. In which the customer are suffers long. So, an online system is badly needed. In this system customer can place their application through online by some clicks. Developer and the authority can manage the customer apply / pre-book and complete validation process through this system. Beside visitor can visit limited page of the site without log in. TCB Products is an intended to make the improvement of taking the products from TCB market. Basically, people taking products of TCB from front of the truck, standing hour and hour. This is very terrifying for people to buy products with standing hour and hour, especially in the summer season. Besides waiting for their essential products people sometimes cannot get their products for shortages of products or products steal by treaders / dealers. In Dhaka city 80% poor people or lower middle-class people are depended to lead their daily needs from TCB Products. Basically, people are buying products from here because of low prices of products. And those products come from government side. That’s cause those products are low cost of price.

TCB (Trading Corporation of Bangladesh) Product project mainly made for lower middle class and poor people. Basically, this type people are buying their daily needs from TCB truck. Per area there has only one truck to provide these products for hundreds of people. This is difficult to provide daily services. Standing front of the truck hour and hour, it wasting people’s time a lot TCB products mainly comes from government side for the poor people and lower middle-class people in low prices. But there are some people always who are want more then enough. From the same family, family members buying products more then enough. Now this Circumnutates are happening daily issue. For this reason, there has more people who can’t buy the products. Beside who are traders, they sometimes do things like dishonest people. Sometimes, they don’t sell the products more. They store the products and doesn’t sell the products to customers. Then after certain time later they sell the stored products too high prices. Almost 90% poor and lower middle-class people are buying products from TCB. Using this website people can booked their products which one they need. In the website we will take NID / Birth certificate number of each family member. Counting the family member, they will get exact measurable products from the TCB Products. From each family, any one person only can go to take all the products of the whole family. When people will book their products, they will also get the date and times when they will get their products. And those all the data will store under government Information. Thus, people can easily get their products without wasting their valuable time. Besides, the traders cannot steal the products or store the products more than enough. Using this website people don’t have to wasting their time, they will get more to income in their professional life.
